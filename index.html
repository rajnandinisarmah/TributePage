DR. SANJUKTA PARASHAR

Key Life Events:

  • IPS Dr. Sanjukta Parashar is the 85th ranker in the All India Civil Services Examination from the 2006 batch. After this, she chose the Meghalaya-Assam cadre.
  • Her first posting was in 2008, Sanjukta Parashar and posted as Assistant Commandant in Makum, Assam. After this, she was sent to control the violence between Bodos and Bangladeshis in Udalgiri.
  • While serving as SP in the Sonitpur district of Assam, Sanjukta Parashar had to lead the team of CRPF jawans. She herself fought with Bodo militants with AK-47 rifles in her hands. Photos of this operation went viral on social media, where she was seen carrying AK-47 rifles with her entire team.
  • Sanjukta Parashar was given death threats by the militant organization for several times , but she never cared about and jumped to fight for the society with all bravery and her name resulted as a nightmare for terrorists.
  • In 2015, Sanjukta Parashar led the anti-Bodo terrorist operation. She had killed 16 militants in just 15 months. Apart from this, she also sent 64 Bodo militants to jail. Along with this, Sanjukta's team had recovered a huge cache of arms and ammunition. Her team had jailed 175 terrorists in 2014 and 172 terrorists in 2013.
  • Apart from being a tough police officer of the society , Sanjukta Parashar spends most of her time helping people in relief camps whenever she gets a break from work. According to her, she is very humble and loving and only criminals should be afraid of her.
